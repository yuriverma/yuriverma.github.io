RaagAI Music Generator

AI Meets Indian Classical Heritage

Project Overview

RaagAI bridges tradition and technology by generating music inspired by Indian classical ragas. With advanced transformer-based models, RaagAI synthesizes melodies that adapt to moods, times of day, and emotions, while preserving the cultural depth of raga structures.

The system not only generates authentic compositions but also provides interactive learning experiences, helping users explore ragas through visualization, notes, and AI-assisted improvisation.
